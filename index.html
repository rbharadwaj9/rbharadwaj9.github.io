About Me

Hi! I'm Rajiv Bharadwaj, a junior at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor.
I'm passionate about robotics and artificial intelligence, and I'm exploring the world of robotic algorithms and embedded systems this fall. I'm part of the Michigan Electric Racing team where I am responsible for the low voltage systems on board an electric racecar! I attend reading groups with the Michigan Student Artificial Intelligence Lab to discuss research papers related to AI. I also am very passionate about music and I sing with the University of Michigan Men's Glee Club, the second oldest collegiate chorus in the United States. I'm also a trained Indian Carnatic Music vocalist and I sing with Michigan Sahana, an Indian Classical music organization.
