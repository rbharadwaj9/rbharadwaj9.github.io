Happy 21st Birthday!!

12.30.2020

Letter

Hey! Happy Birthday Babe! I’m sorry I didn’t plan this out earlier but I was out of ideas as usual and didn’t come to this till the last moment. I know you expected a different experience for your 21st birthday earlier this year but I promise to make it equally great no matter when we do it. I want to bake cakes, bread, and make so much food with you and in the end look forward to you nagging me to exercise (which I like. honestly.) I wish I was there to celebrate this day with you and maybe even meet your friends and family from home (whether online or offline). I will definitely do that soon and plan appropriately. It’s been a weird time for everyone.

However, I want you to think about all the goodness that has come from this year, one of personal growth. You’ve done so well in everything and I genuinely proud of you for it. I’ve seen you grow every moment from the time we met and you inspire me to do the same with myself.

You’ve been asking for a song from me for a long time and I’ve always pushed off anything non classical because it’s never been my forte. However, with growth in mind, I sat in front of the piano and found that no matter how bad I thought I sounded, I still loved doing it. This is what I wanted to share with you. Not something that's been mixed and mastered professonally to sound amazing. I did try to sing it as Perfect-ly as possible but I want this to be a reminder of the impact you’ve had on me and how you’ve motivated me to improve over the times to come and not rely on anything else to make me look good!
Thank you for being there for me always! I look forward to the things we can achieve next year together and come back to this letter and reminisce!

Also, I will need you to get back to your uke…

<3
Rajiv
